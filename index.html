About

NVIDIA Corporation designs and manufactures graphics processing units (GPUs) and system-on-chip units. The company operates through two segments: Graphics and Compute & Networking. Its products serve gaming, professional visualization, data center, and automotive markets. NVIDIA is a leading player in AI chip manufacturing with dominant market share in training GPUs.
